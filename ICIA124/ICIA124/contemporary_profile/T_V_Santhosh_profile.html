INTRODUCTION

(B.1968)
TV Santhosh received his Bachelors degree in Sculpture from Kala Bhavan, Santiniketan, and his Master's degree in Sculpture from M.S. University's Faculty of Fine Arts, Baroda. He is a recipient of the Viswa Bharati Merit Scholarship, Ram Kinkar award and Kanoria Scholarship. Santhosh lives and works in Mumbai.
