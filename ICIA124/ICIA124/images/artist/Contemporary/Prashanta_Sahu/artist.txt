Prashanta Sahu studied at the Kala Bhavan, Vishwa Bharati University, Santiniketan and took his Masters Degree in Fine Art at the M.S. University, Baroda. He received an award from the Eastern Zonal Cultural Center, Kolkata during the 1st Eastern Print Biennale. He was also awarded the National Scholarship of the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India.
